This is my about page.

I am studying in computer science in Penn State Behrend. Also, I am interested in digital art very much, especially in making videos. In ART 168, I have tried many different kinds of digital media, and I found they were difficult but amazing.  I often spent too much time in one project and met many silly problems. Still, solving problems and completing an artwork brought me the sense of achievement.

In 2017, when I watched good promotional videos, I felt excited, so I began to learn how to make a video with Adobe After Effects. After one year, I started to make promotional videos for songs, and they were published by others online. I also makes some short videos for practice.

Now I still work with others to make works together.
